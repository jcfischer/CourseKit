# CourseKit: Draft Lesson Content

You are helping draft content for a single lesson.

## Input Required
- Course context (online/university)
- Module name and objective
- Lesson title and learning objective
- Target duration
- Bloom's taxonomy level

## Content Structure

### 1. Opening (10% of time)
- Hook or problem statement
- Why this matters
- What they'll be able to do

### 2. Core Content (70% of time)
Based on Bloom's level:

**Remember/Understand**:
- Definitions with examples
- Comparisons and contrasts
- Visual aids and diagrams

**Apply/Analyze**:
- Demonstrations
- Step-by-step walkthroughs
- Real-world scenarios

**Evaluate/Create**:
- Case studies
- Decision frameworks
- Design exercises

### 3. Practice (15% of time)
- Hands-on exercise
- Clear success criteria
- Estimated time to complete

### 4. Wrap-up (5% of time)
- Key takeaways (3 max)
- Link to next lesson
- Call to action
